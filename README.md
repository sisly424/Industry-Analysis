# Financial Industry Text Analytics

A data analytics project combining financial statement analysis and natural language processing (NLP) to explore long-term trends, firm performance, and competitive positioning within the U.S. food retail industry.

This project integrates structured financial data with textual disclosures from SEC 10-K filings to identify industry patterns, compare firm performance, and uncover business insights using both quantitative analysis and text mining techniques.

---

## Project Overview

Financial statements describe a company's historical performance, while annual reports provide valuable qualitative information about business strategy, operations, and risk.

This project combines both structured financial data and unstructured 10-K report text to analyze companies in the U.S. food retail industry between 1994 and 2020.

The analysis focuses on industry trends, financial performance, keyword extraction, semantic analysis, and competitor identification to provide a comprehensive view of the sector. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## Business Problem

Investors and business analysts often rely on both financial metrics and corporate disclosures to evaluate companies.

This project addresses several key questions:

- Which companies consistently lead the food retail industry?
- How has the industry evolved over time?
- How did the 2008 financial crisis affect company performance?
- What themes appear most frequently in annual reports?
- Which firms share similar business strategies based on textual disclosures?

By combining financial analytics with NLP techniques, the project demonstrates how structured and unstructured data can support more informed business decisions.

The project uses two primary data sources:

## Project Workflow

1. Filter companies within the Food Store industry using SIC codes.
2. Perform exploratory financial analysis.
3. Analyze long-term industry trends.
4. Evaluate the impact of the 2008 financial crisis.
5. Calculate yearly Return on Assets (ROA).
6. Clean and preprocess 10-K report text.
7. Extract keywords using Word Count and TF-IDF.
8. Generate Word Clouds.
9. Train a Word2Vec model.
10. Identify similar firms through document embeddings.
11. Conduct a case study on Casey's General Stores.

## Financial Analysis

The quantitative analysis includes:

- Top companies ranked by stock price
- Top companies ranked by sales
- Geographic distribution of firms
- Average stock price trends
- ROA trend analysis
- Financial crisis impact analysis

Key findings include:

- Casey's General Stores recorded the highest stock price among food retailers in 2020.
- Kroger consistently ranked among the industry's largest firms by sales.
- The U.S. contains the highest concentration of companies within the selected industry.
- Stock prices experienced substantial declines during the 2008 financial crisis before recovering in subsequent years. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Natural Language Processing

Text preprocessing included:

- Lowercasing
- Punctuation removal
- Stop-word removal

NLP techniques applied:

- Word Frequency Analysis
- TF-IDF
- Word Cloud Visualization
- Word2Vec Embedding

The analysis highlights frequently discussed business topics such as:

- Customers
- Products
- Stores
- Food
- Company strategy

Word2Vec further captures semantic relationships between business concepts and identifies meaningful similarities among corporate disclosures. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

## Competitor Analysis

Using document embeddings generated by Word2Vec, the project identifies companies with similar business characteristics.

A detailed case study was conducted for **Casey's General Stores**, comparing:

- Revenue
- Market Share
- Return on Assets
- Historical Stock Price
- Asset Growth
- Financial Performance

The project also analyzes correlations among stock price, assets, revenue, and profitability to better understand long-term business performance. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

---

## Key Findings

- The U.S. dominates the food retail industry within the dataset.
- Kroger maintained industry leadership in revenue throughout the study period.
- Casey's General Stores demonstrated strong long-term stock price growth.
- Revenue and assets exhibit strong positive correlations with stock price.
- Profitability (ROA) shows greater variability than revenue growth.
- NLP techniques reveal that customer experience, products, and store operations are central themes across annual reports.
- Word2Vec successfully identifies firms with similar business characteristics using textual disclosures. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Continue monitoring asset utilization to improve profitability.
- Combine financial metrics with textual disclosures when evaluating firms.
- Use NLP techniques to identify competitive positioning beyond traditional financial ratios.
- Monitor long-term financial trends instead of relying solely on annual performance.
